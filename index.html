The sticky web.

The ability to monetize the results of design work done for any digital platform has been a difficult proposition. The rampant disregard for copyright, if not the outright theft of intellectual property, is leading to the decline in talent being willing to have works placed on any digital platform. when such placement is viewed as being the equivalent to removing any claims the artist has to work in question. This is leading to the listing of works of art on services such as Getty images and Adobe gallery. As these works are then placed in competition with free images available on the web, The professional artist/stylist will be replaced by whatever can be had.
